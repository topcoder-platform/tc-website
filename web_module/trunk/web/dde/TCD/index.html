Platform Pricing

Which Tier is Right for You?

As you move up in tiers, a larger fixed platform fee is offset by lower variable competition fees – therefore, as you push more through the platform, higher tiers become more cost effective.

To determine which tier is right for you, speak to one of TopCoder’s Sales Professionals.

Training & Mentoring

Our IT organization already has defined best practices. Why do I need to have my team learn more?

Our principles are specific to realizing efficiency in using the TopCoder Platform. Think of it like the Rosetta stone for translating your team’s success in TopCoder.

Is there certification for my staff?

Yes. A TopCoder mentor will be measuring progress and providing feedback throughout the training program so you can be assured your staff is fully prepared to begin taking advantage of the TC Process.

Support & Maintenance

How can a TC Co-Pilot help me?

  • Troubleshooting an issue
  • Launching new contests
  • Manage an upgrade to an existing application delivered by TopCoder, Inc.
  • Help with project planning
  • Run Bug Races to resolve software issues
  • Assist my team with running TopCoder contests

How quick are Bug Races?

They range based on complexity of the issue, but typical durations are under a few hours for trivial issues and less than 2 days for more complex issues.
